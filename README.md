## üé¨ How It Works

### Step 1: Start Recording

Open the PSFlow panel in Photoshop (`Window > Extensions > PSFlow`) and click the record button. It runs silently in the background while you focus on your art.

### Step 2: Create Your Art

Paint, sketch, and design as usual. PSFlow captures every stroke intelligently, pausing automatically when you are idle.

### Step 3: Export Video

Done? Click stop and choose your export settings. Get a high-quality MP4 file instantly, ready to share on social media.

## üõ†Ô∏è Usage Guide

### Recording Your First Timelapse

1. Enable Generator in Photoshop preferences
2. Open the PSFlow panel
3. Click the red **Record** button
4. Start drawing - PSFlow will automatically detect canvas changes
5. Click **Stop** when finished

### Export Settings

- **Recording Intervals**: Minimum time interval for taking snapshots (0 = every action)
- **Duration per Frame**: Time interval between frames in exported video
- **Auto Start Recording**: Automatically start recording when Photoshop launches

### Advanced Features

- **History Tab**: View all recorded sessions with frame count and file size
- **Batch Export**: Export multiple recordings at once
- **Custom Resolution**: Export in various video resolutions
